Big Bang History

-South Korean male band under management of YG Entertainment. -Form by: G-Dragon (Leader), TaeYang (Vice-Leader), TOP, DaeSung, SeungRi. -is known for their unique urban-originated music and fashion style. -the members' involvement in composing and producing their own music, most notably G-Dragon. -Hit song which famous them such as Lie, Haru Haru, Last Farewell, Tonight, Fantastic Baby. - G-Dragon and Taeyang were the first ones to receive training under YG Entertainment at the age of eleven. - They were child models first cast as the younger version of YG artist Jinusean in their music video "A-YO". - The duo released two tracks under the name GDYB, and were often featured in Wheesung, Gummy, Perry, Masta Wu, and Se7en's albums. - As a rapper, G-Dragon also participated in promotional activities with YG Family at fourteen years old. - One of their well-known piece is "Unfold to Higher Place", which also features fellow label mates Perry andGummy. - T.O.P was an underground rapper[7] under the stage name "Tempo". - One of his most popular track was "Buckwild" with NBK Gray. - G-Dragon contacted T.O.P, with whom he was childhood friends, when YG Entertainment was scouting for candidates to create a boy group. - The pair recorded several demos that sent to YG CEO, who later asked T.O.P to audition was rejected because he was deemed too "chubby" to fit the "idealistic version" of an idol. - Six months later, T.O.P was recruited after losing weight as he is reportedly lost twenty kg in forty days.
